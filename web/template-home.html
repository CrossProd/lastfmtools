Welcome

This is my new Last.fm widgets page! Here you can create your own customized widgets for your Last.fm profile.

For feedback or questions you can leave a shout at the group at last.fm.

Info

All the images will update automatically to reflect your latest listening statistics once it is placed on your profile. So there is no need to manually update them. The update frequency depends on what period is chosen and the user status. Premium users have a much better update frequency. You can read more about this in the premium section of this page.

I like the generated widget but how do I place it on my profile?

You have to manually select all the text in the "Code to copy & past" box. Then you have to copy this text to the clipboard. You can do this with the mouse (right mouse button, copy) or by pressing CTRL-C. Then you should edit your last.fm profile and paste the text. You can do this with the mouse (right mouse button, paste) or by pressing CTRL-V.

I tried to place the image(s) on forums, messageboards, etc but it doesn't work. Why?

Some forums have a lot of visitors. This means that the pictures are loaded from the server a lot of times. To protect my server I have decided that the pictures are only allowed on the last.fm profiles.
